ABOUT SCHOOL, ABOUT SURFING, ABOUT THE CANARIES

The Canary Islands are not accidentally called the European Hawaii - it's the perfect place for surfers: stable warm wind all year round, sandy beaches, miles of untouched expanses and clear sea. And the real Mecca for surfers is Fuerteventura - the second largest island in the archipelago.

This is the best place in Spain to take surfing lessons. Apart from the fact that in winter and autumn huge Atlantic waves come here, Fuerteventura also boasts many shallow calm lagoons, perfect for teaching beginners to surf.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available all year round - both waves and suitable weather are always there. And the wave, suitable for the level, will be able to find both real professionals and beginners. It is no coincidence that this place is considered to be the best place for training, where you can easily move to the next level.

When listing the advantages, you cannot avoid the fact that the beaches of Fuerteventura are the longest in the Canaries. As a result, it is impossible to imagine them overcrowded with tourists. You will encounter only those who, like you, will be interested in one goal: learning to surf.

Do you just want to understand what surfing is all about, or have you already experienced all the fun of surfing, but want to raise your level? Our surf school can offer training for all levels.

Four hours of exciting lessons a day will help you confidently conquer the waves, feel like a real surfer.

The main advantages of our Ukrainian school - a high quality of teaching and organizing classes, safety (all our clients are insured in the company Allianz), excellent equipment and equipment (provided for the entire course), including soft safe boards for beginners.

First we determine the level of skiing and physical fitness and only then we make a training program. And we can assure you that once the training is over, not only will you get a lot of great feelings, but also this level will rise to a new level.
